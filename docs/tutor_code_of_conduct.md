# Tutors' Code of Conduct — Home Tuition Nepal

> **Platform:** Home Tuition Nepal · **Operator:** KTM academy
> **Audience:** Every tutor who registers on Home Tuition Nepal.
> **Acceptance:** Mandatory at registration (a dedicated checkbox alongside Terms of Service & Privacy Policy). The acceptance timestamp is stored in `profiles.code_of_conduct_accepted_at`. The full text is also linked from the tutor's Profile → About → Code of Conduct.

---

## Professionalism, ethics and accountability for Home Tuition Nepal tutors

### Standard ethics and etiquette
Tutors at Home Tuition Nepal serve not only as educators but also as role models and mentors. The Tutors' Code of Conduct establishes clear expectations for professional behavior, ethical responsibility, and respectful engagement.

It is designed to uphold the integrity of the tutoring environment, ensure student well-being, and promote a culture of trust, excellence, and accountability. By adhering to these standards, tutors contribute meaningfully to a supportive and effective learning community.

### Professionalism
Tutors should maintain a high level of professionalism in their conduct and interactions with students. This includes being punctual and prepared for tutoring sessions, dressing appropriately, and presenting themselves as knowledgeable and committed educators. Tutors should treat students with respect, patience, and courtesy at all times.

### Confidentiality
Tutors should respect the privacy and confidentiality of their students. They should not disclose any personal or academic information about students without their explicit consent. Tutors should handle student records, grades, and any other sensitive information securely and confidentially. It is important to establish trust and create a safe environment where students feel comfortable sharing their concerns and challenges.

### Competence and knowledge
Tutors should possess the necessary knowledge and expertise in the subject they are tutoring. They should continuously enhance their own knowledge and skills, staying updated with current educational practices, methodologies, and resources. Tutors should provide accurate and up-to-date information to students, ensuring they are well-equipped to support students' learning and academic progress.

### Fairness and impartiality
Tutors should treat all students fairly and impartially, regardless of their background, ethnicity, gender, religion, caste, ability, or any other characteristic. They should not discriminate or show favoritism toward any student. Tutors should provide equal opportunities for all students to succeed, create an inclusive environment, and foster a sense of belonging for every student they tutor.

### Communication
Effective and clear communication is essential for successful tutoring. Tutors should establish open lines of communication with students and parents/guardians, and respond promptly to messages, inquiries, or concerns. Tutors should provide clear instructions, explanations, and feedback to students, ensuring effective communication of the subject matter. They should encourage active participation and engagement from students.

### Integrity
Tutors should act with integrity and honesty in all aspects of their tutoring. They should not engage in any form of academic dishonesty or unethical practices. Tutors should not provide answers or complete assignments on behalf of students. They should encourage independent thinking, critical reasoning, and academic integrity. Tutors should also avoid conflicts of interest and prioritize the best interests of the students they tutor.

### Safety and well-being
Tutors should prioritize the safety and well-being of their students. In offline (in-person) tutoring, tutors should choose suitable locations for tutoring sessions, ensuring a safe and conducive learning environment — preferably the student's home or another agreed safe space. For online tutoring, tutors should use secure platforms and take necessary measures to protect students' personal information and privacy. Tutors should be aware of potential risks and ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

### Boundaries
Tutors should establish clear boundaries in their relationships with students. They should maintain a professional distance and avoid engaging in any behavior that may be perceived as inappropriate or crossing personal boundaries. Tutors **must not** engage in any form of harassment, discrimination, or misconduct. They should create a safe and respectful learning environment for students at all times.

### Continuous professional development
Tutors should engage in ongoing professional development to enhance their teaching skills, knowledge, and effectiveness. They should stay informed about new educational practices, methodologies, technologies, and resources. Tutors should actively seek opportunities for professional growth — workshops, conferences, and relevant training programs. Continuous learning enables tutors to provide the best possible support to their students.

### Compliance with laws and regulations
Tutors should adhere to all applicable laws of Nepal, as well as platform policies set by Home Tuition Nepal and KTM academy. This includes copyright law, data-protection norms, child-safety regulations, and tax obligations on income earned. Tutors should be familiar with these regulations and ensure compliance in their tutoring practices.

### Platform-specific obligations
In addition to the above, every tutor on Home Tuition Nepal agrees to:
- **Never** include phone numbers, WhatsApp links, email addresses, or other external contact details in any free-text field (job descriptions, bid cover notes, vacancy applications, reviews, bio, About sections). The platform's contact-unlock mechanism is the only sanctioned channel for sharing contact details. **Repeated violations result in suspension or permanent ban**, per `plan.md` §5.6.
- Use the **Home Tuition Nepal in-app contact-unlock flow** (or admin-mediated match for vacancies) as the only sanctioned channel for first contact with a student.
- Provide **accurate and verifiable** information during identity verification (citizenship, certificates). Misrepresentation will result in the verified badge being revoked and the account being suspended.
- Treat the student's real name, address, and phone number — revealed only after a confirmed match — as confidential. Do not share or resell this information.
- Honour the agreement reached with the parent / student off-platform. Payment, schedules, and cancellations are between the tutor and the parent; Home Tuition Nepal does not process tuition payments.
- Notify the admin promptly via the in-app *"Contact admin"* link (which opens WhatsApp at `platform_settings.admin_whatsapp`) if a session cannot proceed, a safety concern arises, or a dispute occurs.

### Enforcement
Violation of this Code of Conduct may result in (in increasing severity): an in-app warning, temporary suspension, revocation of the verified badge, permanent ban, and — in cases involving safety, harassment, fraud, or unlawful conduct — referral to the appropriate authorities. All enforcement actions are logged in `audit_events` and accompanied by an in-app notification to the affected tutor with reason and appeal channel.

By tapping **I accept** during registration, the tutor confirms that they have read, understood, and agreed to abide by every provision of this Code of Conduct.

## Become a tutor — How it works for tutors

A short, user-facing onboarding explainer shown on the registration screen (under the *Tutor* role option) and in the public marketing surface (`htn.app/tutors`):

1. **Register** — sign up with your email and phone, choose **I'm a tutor**, accept Terms of Service and the Tutors' Code of Conduct. Verify your phone via OTP.
2. **Build your profile** — pick your **teaching mode** (Online / Offline / Both), the **student levels** you can teach (Below Class 9 / SEE / +2 / A Level), your **subjects and prices** per level, your **weekly availability** (Pre 10 am / 10 am – 5 pm / After 5 pm, by day), and write your **About me / About my sessions / Qualifications** sections.
3. **Verify your identity** — upload your citizenship and academic certificates. Once approved, you get a **Verified ✓** badge and **+50 coins**. Verified tutors are favored in search and stand out on the map.
4. **Get discovered** — students near you find you via the **locality-first map** and the **Find Teachers** list. Admin-curated vacancies (`HTN-NNNNN`) and student-posted jobs are also visible in your Vacancies and Jobs tabs.
5. **Apply** — submit a bid or application for **1 coin** (default; admin-editable). New accounts start with **1000 coins**.
6. **Get hired** — once a student unlocks your contact (or admin assigns you to a vacancy), you'll receive their phone number and real name in-app. Continue the conversation over Call / WhatsApp; agree on schedule and price directly with the parent. **Home Tuition Nepal does not process tuition payments** — all fees are settled off-platform between you and the parent.
7. **Earn reviews** — after each engagement, the student can leave a rating + review. Good reviews boost your visibility and earn you bonus coins.
